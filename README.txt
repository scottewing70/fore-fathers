## 🏌️ What Is This?

The official website for the **Fore! Fathers Golf Club** — a Sunday golf group built on tradition, shit talk, and celebration. This site tracks members, scores, handicaps, weekly leaderboards, tee times, and the photo gallery.

## ✨ Features

- **📋 Player Cards** — Flip each card to view stats, log scores, and edit your profile
- **📊 Auto Handicap** — Calculated automatically from entered round scores (USGA-style differential)
- **🏆 Live Leaderboard** — Weekly results ranked by net score; season standings updated automatically
- **✅ RSVP / Confirmed Status** — Players confirm they're playing Sunday directly from their card
- **☀️ Sunday Weather** — Hourly forecast for Saginaw/Fort Worth, TX (Weather Underground)
- **📷 Photo Gallery** — Members upload photos directly from their phone or computer
- **💾 Persistent Data** — All scores, profiles, RSVPs, and photos save locally in the browser

## 🔄 How to Update the Site

### Option 1 — Ask Copilot (Easiest)
Tell Copilot what to change in chat. Download the new `index.html` and upload it here.

### Option 2 — Edit on GitHub
1. Click `index.html` in this repo
2. Click the ✏️ pencil icon
3. Make your change
4. Click **Commit changes**

### Option 3 — Edit Locally
1. Download `index.html`
2. Open in **VS Code** (free at code.visualstudio.com)
3. Use `Ctrl+F` to find and edit text
4. Re-upload to GitHub

---

## 📤 Deploying Updates

1. Go to this repo on GitHub
2. Click **Add file → Upload files**
3. Drag in the new `index.html`
4. Click **Commit changes**
5. Wait ~30 seconds, then hard refresh: `Ctrl + Shift + R`

## 🏅 Scoring System

- **Handicap** — Calculated from best score differentials (gross score minus course par)
- **Net Score** — Gross score minus handicap index
- **Weekly Winner** — Lowest net score wins; points awarded each round
- **Season Standings** — Total points accumulated across all rounds

---

## 📱 Mobile Friendly

The site works on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop. Members can log scores, confirm RSVP, and upload photos directly from their phone on the course.
