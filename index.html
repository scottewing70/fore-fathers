THE SACRED LAWS OF FORE! FATHERS

  2. 02Mulligans: 1 per round, front 9 only. No mulligan on par 3s.
  3. 03Skins game: $5 per skin. Ties carry over. No cap.
  4. 04Trash talk is encouraged. Crying is not.
  5. 05Every birdie earns a victory lap (or a beer — your call).
  6. 06Sandbagging your handicap will result in public shaming.
  7. 07The 19th hole is mandatory. This is non-negotiable.
  8. 08Lost ball = stroke + distance. No exceptions. We're gentlemen.
  9. 09Slow play = group vote = penalty stroke.
  10. 10The Fore! Fathers group chat is sacred. No muting allowed.
